Jeera to trade in 16430-17330 range

Jeera trading range for the day is 16430 - 17330. Jeera prices gained tracking firmness in spot demand amid lower output from the producing regions. Jeera output in India may fall by 12 percent to 387,000 tons during 2016-17 due to lower acreage and adverse weather conditions. Reports showed that the sowing of Jeera in Gujarat pegged at 278,000 hectare as against 295,000 hectare a year ago.
